About

I am a postdoctoral researcher investigating the economics of silage production when Northern Australia is within drought.

Research Chapters

Chapter 1

What impacts have historical weather events in the Northern Territory/Northern Australia had on cattle sales, domestic market prices and imports and exports of cattle and cattle products in the international beef cattle sector?

Chapter 2

What feeding strategies, including prioritization of cattle developmental stages, silage economics, supplementary nutrients, and herd management practices, enable silage-producing farms to maintain cattle productivity and economic viability during drought conditions?

Chapter 3

Which cattle developmental stage achieves the best production performance and economic outcomes under simulated drought feeding conditions, and how does this affect greenhouse gas emissions?

Chapter 4

What are the characteristics of Northern Territory cattle producers during drought, and what drought management strategies such as feeding, livestock sales, and purchasing decisions are currently in place?
